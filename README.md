**Power-Bi-Sales-Dasboard**
Project Overview
The dashboard provides a complete view of business performance by combining sales analysis operational insights and forecasting to support data driven decision making.

Key Features
KPIs Summary
Total Sales 2M
Total Quantity 22K
Total Profit 175K
Average Delivery Time 10 days

**Sales Analysis**
Sales by category and subcategory
Sales by state and region including Central East South and West
Monthly and yearly sales comparison for 2019 and 2020

**Customer and Operations Insights**
Sales by customer segment including Consumer Corporate and Home Office
Sales by payment mode including COD Online and Cards
Sales by shipping mode including Standard Second Class First Class and Same Day

**Sales Forecasting**
Fifteen day sales forecast using time series analysis
Trend visualization with confidence interval to estimate future sales

**Tools and Technologies**
Power BI Desktop
DAX for calculated measures and KPIs
Power Query for data cleaning and transformation
Built in Power BI forecasting analytics

**Business Value**
Helps identify top performing states categories and subcategories
Supports demand planning and inventory decisions using forecasting
Improves understanding of customer purchasing payment and shipping behavior
Enables faster insights through interactive dashboards and slicers
